Projects

I enjoy craft projects of many kinds: cardmaking, scrapbooking, sewing/machine embroidery, crochet, silversmithing/metal work, printing and really any arty thing I can get my teeth into. I carry out projects in my own time, usually having been inspired by attending a workshop or class showing a particular technique.

Crafty Cards

I create a lot of my cards using supplies from a company called Stampin' Up! These include rubber stamps, punches for cutting out shapes from cardstock and then various embellishemnts such as ribbon, brads, eyelets and gems. I am also a general hoarder, so I use many other bits and pieces I have lying about in my craft stash.

Crafty Crochet

With help from a very kind colleague at work I learnt some basic crochet about 2 years ago now. My two goals at the time were to make a traditional 'granny square' and some sort of flower. I managed both and am currently trying to make a blanket using 'granny stripes'.
